                     English language requirements for exchange students                       

 

All applicants must upload:

  • Official transcript of records in English or a Scandinavian language, listing all courses you have completed at your home university, including number of credits and grades.
  • Or, if the above is not available: A translation of your transcript of records in English or a Scandinavian language in addition to an official transcript of records in the original language. We accept a non-official translation if stamped and signed by an employee at the partner university.
  • Passport or national ID card

 

Some applicants must upload:

  • Letter of motivation: If you apply for courses in English language and literature at the Faculty of Humanities (Course codes ENG[xxx]).
  • Traineeship Agreement: Applies to Erasmus+ Traineeship students. The Traineeship Agreement is issued by your home university and must be signed by all three parties.
  • Documentation of funds: Applies to students from non-EU/EEA/EFTA countries.
  • Documentation of proficiency in Norwegian: If you apply for courses taught in Norwegian (NOT Norwegian language courses)

 

Erasmus+ applicants can also upload:

  • Learning Agreement: We recommened that you only upload a Learning Agreement if you need it signed before you arrive in Bergen. The Learning Agreement is issued by your home university.
